We're Dev Saber - RobôChicas

RoboChicas is a robotics club of the Institute of Science, Technology and Innovation (ICTI) of the Federal University of Bahia (UFBA), located in the municipality of Camaçari.

This club aims to build an environment to promote the training and qualification of women (mainly black and socioeconomically vulnerable) in the city of Camaçari in the areas of Science, Technology and Engineering (CT&E).
